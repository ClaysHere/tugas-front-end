About AnimalPedia

We are AnimalPedia.

Leading the world to a better ecosystem.

About Us

AnimalPedia is a website dedicated to raise awareness about endangered species and conserving the ecosystem. We believe that everyone can help make the earth a better place again, one human at a time. We are here to serve justice for animals who don't deserve to extinct.

Our Vision and Mission

01

Make the Earth Green Again

We are doing our best to plant as many trees as possible around the globe and expanding the habitats of endangered species. We are very grateful for all the donations that we have received.

02

Diversifying the Globe

To prevent more species going extinct, we are doing all the best we can to protect every animal. Our planet has been gifted with so much diversity of creatures, and we must make it stay that way.

03

Balancing the Ecosystem

With more and more animals going extinct, the food chain is starting to fall off. By saving these animals, we are going to keep the food chain in place.

04

Be their voice

We aim to be the mediator between animals and humans. We believe that animals deserve the same amount of right as us humans do.
